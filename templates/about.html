About Me

This is who I am.

The journey of a thousand miles begins with a single step but the journey of a thousand miles also ends with a single step and as such I take a step at a time knowing that one day I will finally be stepping into my dreams. They say Rome was not built in a day but one day Rome was built. I believe I am building something greater because I have a God greater than the Roman Emperor. People often tell me not to give up, but they are wrong because everyone has to give up at a point in life. The only thing that matters is what you give up. You can either give up on sleep or give up on your dreams. While others are sleeping and dreaming about things chasing them, I prefer to stay awake and chase my dreams. Despite all this, without God, I am nothing, so I give God all the glory. This is who I am.
